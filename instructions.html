Start Designing in 3 quick steps!

Step 1:

Clicking “Start New Design” takes you to our auto-scaling page where we will calculate the size of your dorm and adjust furniture to scale.

Step 2:

Use our drag-and-drop feature to add furniture to your dorm. Here you can add, delete, and rotate all kinds of furniture.

Step 3:

If you log in, you can save and load floor plan designs on our website. You can also share floor plans with friends to work together!

We recommend using Chrome for the best experience.
